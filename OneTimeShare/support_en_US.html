How to Use the App

  1. Launch the app.
  2. Choose a photo saved on your iPhone.
  3. Tap the "Generate QR Code" button to upload the photo to the cloud.
  4. A URL will be automatically generated and shown as a QR code.
  5. Show the QR code to someone so they can download the photo via the URL.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q. How long is the QR code URL valid?

A. The URL is valid for approximately 24 hours after it is generated. Once an image has been downloaded, the URL is no longer valid.

Q. Can anyone who knows the URL access the photo?

A. Yes. Anyone who knows the URL can access the photo. Please be careful when sharing it, especially for private or sensitive images.

Q. Can I delete a photo after it's been uploaded?

A. Currently, there is no feature to manually delete an uploaded photo. The URL will automatically expire after its validity period ends.

Q. Where are the images stored? Is it secure?

A. The upload destination is Cloudflare Workers KV. The images are encrypted and transmitted, so they are not available on the cloud service side. The encryption key will be in the fragment part of the URL, and the image will be decrypted on the client side. The encryption key is not sent to the server (E2E encryption). The public URL can be accessed only once by anyone who knows it.

Q. Can I print the QR code or share it via apps like LINE?

A. Absolutely. You can share the QR code however you like. Just remember that anyone with the URL will be able to access the photo.

Notes

  • The URL is only valid for a limited time and will become inaccessible after the expiration period.
  • Since anyone with the URL can view the photo, please be mindful of privacy when sharing.
  • Depending on your internet connection or iOS settings, the app may not function properly in some environments.
