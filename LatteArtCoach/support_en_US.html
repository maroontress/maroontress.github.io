Welcome!

This page provides answers to common questions, usage instructions, and how to contact support for the Latte Art Rating App.

How to Use the App

  1. Launch the app.
  2. Select a latte art photo from your iPhone.
  3. Tap the "Review" button to upload the photo for AI analysis.
  4. The feedback and score will appear instantly.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q. How do I use the app?

A. Simply open the app and select a latte art photo from your camera roll. The AI will automatically analyze and score your art.

Q. How is the evaluation done?

A. Our AI, powered by OpenAI technology, evaluates your latte art based on factors such as symmetry, contrast, and overall composition. It provides feedback on both strengths and areas for improvement.

Q. Why is an internet connection required?

A. The image analysis is performed in the cloud, so an active internet connection is necessary.

Q. What does the score mean?

A. The app gives a score out of 100 based on multiple aspects like balance, clarity, and consistency of your latte art.
